## Interacting with the Terrain

### Mouse Controls
- **Rotate**: Left-click and drag anywhere on the terrain
- **Zoom**: Scroll your mouse wheel up (zoom in) or down (zoom out)
- **Pan**: Right-click and drag to move the view

### Keyboard (Browser Default)
- **F11**: Toggle fullscreen (in most browsers)
- **Ctrl + 0**: Reset zoom (browser level)

## Tips for Best Experience

1. **Rotate the view** to see the mountains from different angles
2. **Zoom in** close to see individual trees and terrain details
3. **Look for the red flags** marking the highest peaks
4. **Notice the color gradient** from green lowlands to white peaks

## Troubleshooting

### Black screen or no terrain?
- **Check console**: Press F12 ‚Üí Console tab
- **WebGL support**: Ensure your browser supports WebGL
- **Try a different browser**: Chrome usually has best support

### Slow performance?
- **Close other tabs** to free up GPU memory
- **Reduce browser zoom**: Set to 100%
- **Update graphics drivers** if very slow

### Trees or markers not visible?
- **Zoom out** a bit - they might be behind the camera
- **Rotate the view** - they're placed on specific elevations

## Taking Screenshots

- **Peaks**: Focus on red flag markers
